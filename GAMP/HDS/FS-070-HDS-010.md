# FS-070-HDS-010 Per-VM Activation-policy Inputs

Each instance independently supplies boot-start, guest-restart, guest candidate
selection, explicit-start candidate selection, and inclusive jitter bounds.
Invalid combinations or bounds are rejected while evaluating the host.

Named host carrier policies supply a carrier interface, selected VM instances,
additional required host-local interfaces, a positive polling interval, and a
dry-run flag. The generated policy service is ordered after all declared device
units and delegates typed automatic start and stop authority to the manager.

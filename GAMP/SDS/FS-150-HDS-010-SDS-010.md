# FS-150-HDS-010-SDS-010 Stable tmux Console Adapter

The lifecycle supervisor launches the exact selected runner in a detached tmux
session at the configured per-VM socket, records the pane process identifier,
and observes pane liveness and exit status. Start, rollout, guest restart, and
rollback reuse the same socket and session settings. Service stop terminates
the runner, kills its session, and removes the socket. Functional health remains
a separate mandatory check.
